Follow The Project

Poker Monsters was originally born as a Print & Play board game, but when we noticed its potential, we decided to transform it into a video game.

Me and my teammates firmly believe in this project and we will continue its development during our spare time. We are currently looking for a publisher, but even if we don't find it, we will release the full game sooner or later.
